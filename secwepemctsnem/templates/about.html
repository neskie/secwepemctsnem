About

This website is a place to help you learn Secwepemctsin. It is designed for novice speakers to fluent speakers. You can add your own audio for words, sentences and phrases.

Adding Words

Add new words that aren't in the list. You can add audio files to words that don't already have them. If you need a digital recorder you can use the mic on your computer or laptop or you could purchase one. Transom has a good comparision chart.

You can record your voice using a program called Audacity. It is free and available for Windows, Mac and Linux.

This site was developed using Django. You can download the Source code on github.
